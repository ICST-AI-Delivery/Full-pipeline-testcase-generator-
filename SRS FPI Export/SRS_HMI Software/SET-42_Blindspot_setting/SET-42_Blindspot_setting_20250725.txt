1. ID: 3620010
- Contents: This setting allows the user to enable the blind spot monitor to get an information about near obstacles in vehicle blind spots.
The blind spot sensors detect other highway licensable vehicles that enter the vehicle's blind spot detection zone while vehicle is in drive gear. In addition, the sensors incorporate a Rear Cross Traffic Alert (RCTA) function that will alert the driver to oncoming vehicles that may hit the host vehicles while the vehicle is in reverse gear
