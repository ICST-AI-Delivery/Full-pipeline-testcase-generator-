### Informational Items:

1. ID: 4032636
- Contents: The Audio Preset feature allows the user to select between the following audio presets:
Studio preset allows the listener to listen to music with the highest fidelity in terms of Timbre, Definition, and Frequency Response;
Concerto preset allows the listener to listen to music tracks as in a live experience enhancing Dynamics, Sound Image and perceived listening environment size;
Cinema preset enhances the auditory experience and the soundscape, improving depth of perceived sounds by enlarging the Sound Image without distorting the Timbre;
Immersive presets allows the listener to be at the center of the sound event, creating a three-dimensional Sound Image;
Comfort preset reduces all the most tiring components of listening, minimizing the effort and attention required, making it ideal for long trips or non-active listening.
- Artifact Type: Information
- State (Harman_Requirement): New
