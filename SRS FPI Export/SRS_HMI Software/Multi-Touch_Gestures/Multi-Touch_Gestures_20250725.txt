3. ID: 2354654
- Contents: 6.1.2.3.3 The System shall distinguish the following gestures at a low-level processing la...



The System shall distinguish the following gestures at a low-level processing layer within the touchscreen:

Pinch/Stretch: Touch surface with two fingers and bring them closer together / move them apart (e.g., zoom map)

Rotate: Touch surface with two fingers and move them in a clockwise or counterclockwise direction (e.g., rotate pictures, rotate the map)

Drag: Move with 2 or 3 fingers over the surface without losing contact (e.g., move to previous/next item in a list
- Artifact Type: SRS Req
- Responsible Domain: Android HMI
