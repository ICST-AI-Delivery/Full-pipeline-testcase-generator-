1. ID: 3927160
- Contents: The system shall follow the below behavior when parking sound feedback is 'active' & the obstacle detection by sensors.
There shall be no obstacles detected when vehicle moves away from the obstacle.
IDC switches to parking-view when an obstacle is detected under threshold.
The Parking-view shall not-be displayed if Reverse-gear is engaged & a front-obstacle is detected.
- Artifact Type: SRS Req
- Responsible Domain: Camera
- Test Stage: System Qualification Test
- Verification Criteria: Precondition:
IDC screen is displayed, parking sound feedback is already active and there are obstacles detected by sensors

Verify the below behavior.
1. Vehicle moves away from the obstacle, there are no obstacles detected, IDC screen is still displayed.
2. for both Front and Rear, IDC screen switches to Parking view when an obstacle is detected under threshold.

3. If Reverse gear is engaged, with IDC screen visualized, and a front obstacle is detected the Parking view shall not be displayed.
