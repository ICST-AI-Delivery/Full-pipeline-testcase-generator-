1. ID: 3712898
- Contents: The BSW has Acoustic Directional Feedback and it shall be operational when an object presence is detected into the Blind spot of the side the turn indicators are currently ON.

The RCTA has Acoustic Feedback and it shall be operational when the presence of oncoming crossing traffic is detected in the rear area of the vehicle.
