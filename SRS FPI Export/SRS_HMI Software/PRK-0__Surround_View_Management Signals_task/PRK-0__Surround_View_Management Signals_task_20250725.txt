### Informational Items:

1. ID: 3841120
- Contents: - Below are listed all the signals received by the IDC for SW management and the task assigned:
<VehicleSpeedVSOSIg>, <VehicleSpeedVSOSIgFailSts>, <FutureSyncGear> are used, together with
timer, to manage different views
<LwsAngle>, <LWSFailSts> are used to calculate the direction of front/rear dynamic parking lines
<STS_DRS_DriverDoorSts> (and PROXI parameter Side Driver), <STS_DRS_PsngrDoorSts>,
<STS_DRS_RearRightDoorSts>,
<STS_DRS_RearLeftDoorSts> (if PROXI parameter Rear_Doors = present) are used to detect if a door
is opened and manage the HMI view
<HighBeamSts>, <LowBeamSts>, <NightDaySts> <BrakePedalSts> <turnIndicatorSts>
<StopLightActRqt> <LHStopLightFailSts> <RHStopLightFailSts> <PLGSts> <HazardActRqt>
<RechargeFlapSts> <Left_PL_Feedback> <Right_PL_Feedback> <LHParkTailLightSts>
<RHPArkLLightSts> are used to update the HMI views
<Alert_RCTA> is used by IDC to show the visual alert
<FoldMirrorSts> is used by IDC to know the status of mirrors (open/closed)
<STATUS_NSP2> is used by IDC to manage visualization of front/rear virtual walls
<YRS_DATA> <ESP_WSS_Front> <ESP_WSS_Rear> are additional information for the Surround view
