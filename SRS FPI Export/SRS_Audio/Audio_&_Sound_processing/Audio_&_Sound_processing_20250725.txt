### Primary Requirements:

1. ID: 2357081
- Contents: 6.1.3.1.1.1.6.1 The System shall support the following list of audio sources: Entertainment and Connectivity audio. E,g., navigation, voice assistant, telephony, etc.Call audio. E.g., real-time streaming from connected phones, etc.Acoustics. E.g., vehicle sounds (parking, welcome/goodbye, etc.), user sounds (touches, notifications, etc.), cluster sounds (warnings, status notifications, etc.), etc.Tuner audio. E.g., streaming from Tuner device, etc.Early and vehicle audio. E.g., tone generators and predefined players, etc.Microphone audio in. E.g., in-vehicle microphone inputs, including the reference channels to able ECNR or provide Media Clock Reference, etc.
