The radio equipment shall:

 

include elements to monitor and control network traffic, including the transmission of outgoing data

be designed to mitigate the effects of ongoing denial of service attacks

implement appropriate authentication and access control mechanisms

be provided, on a risk basis, with up-to-date software and hardware at the moment of placing on the market that do not contain publicly known exploitable vulnerabilities as regards harm to the network or its functioning or misuse of network resources

be provided with automated and secure mechanisms for updating software or firmware that allow, when necessary, the mitigation of vulnerabilities that if exploited may lead to the radio equipment harming the network or its functioning or the misuse of network resources

protect the exposed attack surfaces and minimise the impact of successful attacks
